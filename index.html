A Little About Me

I am currently majoring in Computational Modeling & Data Analytics at Virginia Tech. I am also minoring in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Cyber Security. When I am not working on school work, I like to spend my time doing a variety of things. I absolutley love playing soccer, as I have been playing my entire life. I also like working on personal computer science related projects, since actively building new projects helps me learn new languages, frameworks, and concepts better. Even though I have a passion for technology, I also love art and desgin, which is way I'm always designing new logos, websites, drawings, etc. Front end development definitely interests me the most since I am able to combine computer science and design. To help me unwind from the stresses of school, I enjoy playing the ukulele and messing around on the piano. When school is on break and I get to go home, I am either spending time with my dog, Hudson, or catching up with my friends and family.

Activities

The mission of Rocketry at Virginia Tech is to design, construct, and launch a rocket to 10,000 ft while competing at the Spaceport America Cup against other collegiate design teams. My role on the team is Data Scientist, which consists of retrieving, analyzing, and visualizing data from test launches in order to optimize the rocket's performance. Although I work with data a lot, I also help out with other programming tasks, like building the team's website. Even though this team requires a lot of my time, it is loads of fun since I am able to take what I learn in the classroom and apply in a real-world setting to help my team.

Art and graphic design has always been a huge part of my life. Before I figured out that I loved computer science junior year of high school, I was planning on going to college to study graphic design. Everyday in high school, I spent hours in independent studies and study halls working on design projects. I even competed and placed in several design competeitions. I have uploaded what I consider my best work on my online portfolio. I encourage you to check it out!
